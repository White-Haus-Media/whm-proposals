02
The Opportunity

The Organization Has Outgrown
Its Digital Home.

We say this as creative partners, not critics: the digital experience hasn't yet caught up to the organization behind it. That's one of the most exciting growth levers NSN has right now — and the kind of opportunity we'd love to build into alongside you.

There's a principle we believe in across every nonprofit engagement we touch: when your digital platform is working for you, the whole organization can stop working harder than it needs to. A site that earns its keep takes pressure off the team, off the calendar, and off the conference cycle.

There's already a membership page and a member login experience in place — and we want to be clear that NSN's foundation here is real. The opportunity is to bring that experience fully into the NSN brand and platform. Today, the join and login flows route members out to Memberclicks, a third-party membership system. The next evolution is an on-brand, on-domain experience that demonstrates the value of membership before the click, retains members between events, gives the 70+ sponsor partners a year-round commercial surface, and gives the 100,000+ people in the network a reason to engage digitally even when there's no conference on the calendar.

Where Things Stand Today

Sponsors complete a form before they can see tiers or packages
A decade of conference video and media lives one layer deep

What This Becomes

This isn't about aesthetics — it's about revenue. A modern digital platform is a commercial decision, and it shapes how many people join, how many renew, how many sponsors commit at higher tiers, and how many of the 100K+ contacts in the network become paying members. The opportunity in front of NSN is to close the space between the caliber of what this team has built over three decades and how that story lives online. That's where the next chapter of growth begins — and it's the work we'd love to partner with you on.

03
The Vision

Three Pillars. One Platform.

The new NSN digital experience is built around three core pillars — each one tied directly to a revenue outcome and a member outcome. Together, they turn the website from a brochure into the operational backbone of the organization.

Pillar 01
The Member Experience
A fully realized member hub that brings the existing membership experience back into the NSN brand — and gives professionals a reason to visit, engage, and stay 365 days a year, not just during conference season.
  • On-brand member portal — Memberclicks data integrated into a native NSN experience, no external handoff
  • Resource library — on-demand sales training, career tools, exclusive content
  • Member directory — searchable network of 100K+ professionals
  • Chapter events calendar — all 20 chapters, unified in one place
  • Member spotlight hub — the chapter-level stories that currently only live on Instagram, now amplified on the website
  • Tiered membership — entry-level, professional, and executive tiers that reduce friction to join and increase lifetime value
Pillar 02
The Sponsor Engine
A year-round commercial platform that gives corporate partners ongoing value — not just a conference weekend. The site becomes a revenue surface that runs independently of the event calendar.
  • Live sponsor hub — transparent tiers, ROI data, real audience demographics
  • Self-serve packages — lower-tier sponsors can select, pay, and activate without a sales call
  • Digital ad inventory — newsletter sponsorships, homepage placements, content series branding
  • Job board — employers post directly to the NSN member base; hiring outcomes tracked and reported
  • Partner case studies — how Salesforce, Microsoft, and J&J have used NSN to diversify their sales organizations
Pillar 03
The Growth Infrastructure
The backend systems that make the entire organization easier to scale — new chapters, new markets, new members — without adding operational overhead.
  • Chapter page system — 20 templated, SEO-optimized local landing pages; new chapters deploy in hours
  • Local search capture — "sales networking events in [city]" routes to the NSN chapter page in that market
  • Conference bridge — seamless path from conference registration to membership, no drop-off
  • Sales Influencer program integration — the referral program gets a dedicated acquisition funnel
  • 10+ years of media surfaced — conference videos, photos, testimonials become homepage conversion assets
