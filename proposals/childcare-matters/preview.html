A Nonprofit Model Built by Parents, for Parents

Childcare Matters is Chapel Hill's longest-tenured childcare center, guided by a parent-led board and a team of educators committed to learning through play.

We believe childhood is about wonder, exploration, and connection. Our award-winning program honors that with low teacher-to-child ratios, stability, and a learning environment that grows with your child.

What makes us different:

  • Parent-led governance — your voice shapes our community
  • Longest teaching tenure in Chapel Hill — consistency matters
  • Small class sizes — 7 to 12 children per classroom
  • Learning through play — not worksheets
  • Nonprofit mission — every dollar serves the children
