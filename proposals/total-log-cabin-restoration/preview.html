Full Service Restoration

Everything your log home needs, under one roof.

From the first inspection to the final coat of stain, we handle every step of the restoration process with the same attention to detail.

Repair & Restoration

Structural repairs, log replacement, and comprehensive restoration bringing your cabin back to its original strength and beauty.

Chinking & Caulking

Proper sealing that keeps moisture, insects, and air infiltration out while maintaining the authentic character of your log home.

Pressure Washing

Professional cleaning that removes years of weathering, mold, mildew, and debris — the essential first step in any restoration.

Stain Application

Exterior and interior stain applied with precision — protecting the wood for years to come while enhancing its natural character.

Inspections & Testing

pH level testing, moisture testing, and full inspections that catch problems early and guide the right restoration approach.

Maintenance Programs

Annual and seasonal maintenance plans that protect your investment long after the restoration is complete.

Insect Prevention

Treatment that protects log structures from wood-boring insects, termites, and other pests that can compromise structural integrity.

Deck Sealers & Stains

Deck restoration and sealing services that extend the life of your outdoor spaces and keep them looking their best season to season.
