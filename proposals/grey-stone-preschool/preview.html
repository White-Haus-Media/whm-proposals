A Home Away from Home

Nestled in the heart of Old West Durham inside Grey Stone Church, we provide a nurturing, academically enriching environment where young learners thrive. Our half-day model gives families flexibility while ensuring children receive world-class instruction and individualized attention.

We've been recognized twice by Indy Week as Durham's Best Early-Childhood Facility because we don't just educate—we cultivate confidence, creativity, and a love of learning that lasts a lifetime.

Our Programs

Ages 2–3

Toddler-focused curriculum with sensory play, music, and early language development. Small class sizes ensure each child gets one-on-one attention.

Ages 3–4

Preschool program emphasizing letters, numbers, and social-emotional skills. Outdoor play, art, and science exploration daily.

Ages 4–5

Pre-K curriculum preparing children for kindergarten. Phonics, mathematics, writing readiness, and enrichment activities.

Kindergarten

Full curriculum in same 9 AM–12 PM timeframe. Reading, math, science, and critical thinking for rising 1st graders.

Lunch Bunch

Extend your child's day. 12–12:50 PM supervised lunch and free play. Perfect for families needing a few extra minutes.

Summer Camps

Extended summer programs featuring themed activities, field trips (virtual and in-person), and skill-building fun.

Why Families Choose Grey Stone

Award-Winning Excellence

Named Indy Week's Best Early-Childhood Facility in 2018 and 2021. Our reputation speaks to our commitment to quality instruction and care.

Unbeatable Location

Steps from Duke University, easy access via Highway 15-501 and I-85. Old West Durham puts us near families who value community and proximity.

Flexible Half-Day Model

9 AM–12 PM programs give families time for work, errands, or other kids. Lunch Bunch extends the day when you need it. No full-day requirement.
