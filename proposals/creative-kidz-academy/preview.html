Our Story

A family-owned center built on trust

Creative Kidz Academy has been a cornerstone of the North Raleigh community since 2004. Located at 8408 Strickland Road, our privately owned and operated center provides a warm, structured environment where children thrive.

Owner and on-site administrator Christy Lewis leads a team committed to developmentally appropriate practices. Over ninety percent of our staff hold lead teacher credentials or a degree in early childhood education — a standard that sets us apart.

Programs

Care designed for every stage

From infants to elementary schoolers, each program is built around where your child is developmentally — and where they are headed next.

6 weeks - 12 months

Infant Care

Nurturing, one-on-one attention in a safe environment. Individualized schedules for feeding, napping, and play that follow each baby's natural rhythm. Our infant rooms are designed to feel like a home away from home.

1 - 2 years

Toddler Program

Hands-on exploration and early social development. Language-rich activities that spark curiosity, build confidence, and lay the groundwork for the structured learning ahead.

3 - 5 years

Certified Preschool

State-certified curriculum preparing children for kindergarten readiness. Our preschool program blends academic foundations with creative expression, building the skills and confidence children need to excel.

5 - 12 years

Before & After School

Safe transport from five local elementary schools with structured homework time and enrichment activities. We make the transition between school and home seamless for working families.

All ages

Summer Camp & Track-Out

Themed weeks packed with field trips, arts, sports, and hands-on learning all summer long. Our track-out programs keep kids engaged and growing during school breaks throughout the year.

Why Creative Kidz

What families value most

Privately Owned, Personally Invested

This is not a franchise. Creative Kidz Academy is independently owned and operated by Christy Lewis, which means every decision is made with your children in mind — not a corporate office.

Credentialed Teaching Staff

Over ninety percent of our teachers hold lead teacher status or a degree in early childhood education. Your child learns from professionals who have invested in their craft.

Continuity from Infancy to Elementary

Children can grow with us from 6 weeks through age 12 — infant care, preschool, before and after school, track-out, and summer camp all in one trusted place.

Built-In School Transportation

We provide daily transport to and from Millbrook, Leadmine, Abbots Creek, Lynn Road, and North Ridge Elementary — one less thing for working parents to coordinate.

Two Decades of Community Trust

Since 2004, Raleigh families have chosen Creative Kidz Academy. That kind of longevity speaks to the relationships we build and the care we provide every single day.

WakeMed Employee Partnership

A proud partner in the WakeMed Health and Hospitals Employee Discount Program, making quality child care more accessible for local healthcare workers and their families.
