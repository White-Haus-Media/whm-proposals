Our Story

A family-owned center built on trust

Creative Kidz Academy has been a cornerstone of the North Raleigh community since 2004. Located at 8408 Strickland Road, our privately owned and operated center provides a warm, structured environment where children thrive from their earliest days through elementary school.

Our team uses developmentally appropriate practices to guide every child's growth. Over ninety percent of our lead teachers are credentialed or hold a degree in early childhood education — a commitment to quality that families across Wake County have trusted for more than two decades.

Programs

Care designed for every stage

From infants to elementary schoolers, each program is built around where your child is developmentally — and where they are headed next.

6 weeks – 12 months

Infant Care

Nurturing, one-on-one attention in a safe environment. Individualized schedules for feeding, napping, and play that follow each baby's natural rhythm.

1 – 2 years

Toddler Program

Hands-on exploration and early social development. Language-rich activities that spark curiosity and build confidence during these foundational years.

3 – 5 years

Certified Preschool

State-certified curriculum preparing children for kindergarten readiness through structured learning, creative play, and social skill building.

5 – 12 years

Before & After School

Safe transport from five local elementary schools with structured homework time, enrichment activities, and supervised outdoor play.

All ages

Summer Camp

Themed weeks packed with field trips, arts, sports, and hands-on learning all summer long. A summer they will remember.

Why Creative Kidz

What families value most

Two decades of trust, built one family at a time.

Privately Owned, Personally Invested

This is not a franchise. Creative Kidz Academy is independently owned and operated, which means every decision is made with your children in mind — not a corporate office.

Credentialed Teaching Staff

Over ninety percent of our teachers hold lead teacher status or a degree in early childhood education. Your child learns from professionals who have invested in their craft.

Continuity from Infancy to Elementary

Children can grow with us from 6 weeks through age 12 — infant care, preschool, before and after school, track-out, and summer camp all in one trusted place.

Built-In School Transportation

We provide daily transport to and from Millbrook, Leadmine, Abbots Creek, Lynn Road, and North Ridge Elementary — one less thing for working parents to coordinate.

Two Decades of Community Trust

Since 2004, Raleigh families have chosen Creative Kidz Academy. That kind of longevity speaks to the relationships we build and the care we provide every single day.

Full Flexibility for Working Families

Full-time, part-time, before and after school, track-out, summer camp. We designed our programs around the real schedules of working parents in North Raleigh.
