Our Story

A family-owned center built on trust

Creative Kidz Academy has been a cornerstone of the North Raleigh community since 2004. Located at 8408 Strickland Road, our privately owned and operated center provides a warm, structured environment where children thrive.

Our team uses developmentally appropriate practices (DAP) to guide every child's growth. Over ninety percent of our staff have earned lead teacher status or a degree in early childhood education — a commitment to quality that sets us apart.

Programs

Care designed for every stage

From infants to elementary schoolers, each program is built around where your child is developmentally — and where they are headed next.

6 weeks - 12 months

Infant Care

Nurturing, one-on-one attention in a safe environment. Individualized schedules for feeding, napping, and play that follow each baby's natural rhythm.

1 - 2 years

Toddler Program

Hands-on exploration and early social development. Language-rich activities that spark curiosity and build confidence.

3 - 5 years

Preschool

State-certified curriculum preparing children for kindergarten readiness.

5 - 12 years

Before & After School

Safe transport from 5 local elementary schools with structured homework time and enrichment activities.

All ages

Summer Camp

Themed weeks packed with field trips, arts, sports, and hands-on learning all summer long.
