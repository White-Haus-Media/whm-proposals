The Practice

What Is a Sound Bath

A deeply immersive, full-body listening experience that harnesses the power of sound vibrations to promote physical, emotional, and mental well-being.

During a sound bath, you are enveloped in a soothing and harmonious atmosphere created by a variety of sound healing instruments. These instruments produce a rich tapestry of frequencies that wash over you — slowing brain waves, reducing cortisol, and guiding your body into its deepest state of rest and repair.

It is not a performance. It is a surrender. You simply lie down, close your eyes, and allow the vibrations to carry you somewhere you can't reach on your own.
