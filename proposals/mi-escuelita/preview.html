Our Immersion Philosophy

Every child deserves to be bilingual. Our approach honors home languages while building English proficiency.

Bilingual Pathways

Mi Escuelita serves two distinct populations: Spanish-speaking children maintain and strengthen their native language while developing English skills, and English-speaking children build foundational Spanish proficiency.

This dual approach creates a naturally bilingual classroom where languages are tools for learning and connection.

"Literacy in both Spanish and English opens doors. We prepare children not just for kindergarten, but for a bilingual future."
