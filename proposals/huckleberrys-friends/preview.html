Our Story

Building Foundations Since 1987

Huckleberry's Friends was founded on a simple belief: every child deserves an environment where they can grow academically, emotionally, and socially. For nearly four decades, we've been that place for families in Garner.

We're not just a daycare—we're a partnership between families and educators who believe in going above and beyond state standards. Our family-owned approach means every decision is made with your child's development in mind.

We exceed expectations. Our curriculum blends structured academics with play-based learning, our facility is beautiful and safe, and our staff treats every child as part of the family. That's why families choose us, and why they stay.
