Our Story

Built by a mother who believes every child is gifted

Gifted Stars Early Learning Academy was founded by Jasmine Horton, a mother of two who devoted her career to early childhood education. Her vision was simple: create a learning environment where children are nurtured through a curriculum that blends academics, creativity, and real-world exploration.

Today, Gifted Stars operates three campuses across North Carolina, serving families from Chapel Hill to Hickory. Every classroom maintains a 1:6 teacher-to-child ratio, and every child receives instruction in STEM, sign language, Spanish, yoga, and more.

The Horton Experience

A curriculum that goes beyond the classroom

Every week is themed with hands-on learning, creative exploration, and real-world experiences that prepare children for kindergarten and beyond.

STEM Learning

Science experiments, number play, and hands-on building activities woven into every themed week. Children learn through discovery and problem-solving.

Bilingual Foundations

Spanish instruction and toddler sign language are part of the daily routine, giving children early tools for communication and cognitive development.

Movement & Mindfulness

Outdoor yoga sessions, soccer, and structured play develop gross motor skills while teaching children to focus, breathe, and regulate their emotions.

Cooking & Nutrition

Weekly cooking classes teach children about healthy eating, measurements, and following directions while creating dishes they can share with classmates.

Creative Arts

Daily arts and crafts projects culminating in a semester art show. Children express themselves through painting, drawing, sculpting, and mixed media.

Field Trips

Regular excursions to the Museum of Life and Science, local farms, pumpkin patches, and movie theaters expand learning beyond the classroom walls.

Programs

Care designed for every stage

From infants through pre-K, each classroom is structured around developmental milestones and guided by our STEM-based curriculum.

6 months - 1 year

Infant Classroom

Gentle, nurturing care with sensory exploration, early sign language, and individualized routines. Each infant receives one-on-one attention in a safe, stimulating environment.

Why Gifted Stars

What families value most

01

Owner-Operated, Mother-Founded

Gifted Stars is not a franchise. Jasmine Horton built this academy as a mother who wanted more for her own children. That personal investment shows in every classroom decision.

02

1:6 Teacher-to-Child Ratio

Small class sizes mean your child is never lost in a crowd. Every teacher knows every child by name, by personality, and by developmental needs.

03

A Curriculum You Cannot Find Elsewhere

STEM learning, toddler sign language, Spanish, outdoor yoga, cooking classes, and weekly field trips. This is not a standard daycare schedule.

04

Real-Time Parent Communication

A dedicated parent app provides live updates — photos, videos, diaper changes, feedings, and direct messaging with teachers throughout the day.

05
