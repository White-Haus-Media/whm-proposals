What We Found

We reviewed woodallsfitness.com directly. Here's what we found — no assumptions, just what's live on the site today.

Your content lives on someone else's platform
Every "Strength Report" article on your site links out to conta.cc — Constant Contact's domain. Readers leave your site, Google doesn't credit woodallsfitness.com for any of it, and you're building someone else's traffic instead of your own. Twenty years of expertise should be searchable on your domain.
No online booking — a contact form is not a signup flow
Someone ready to join has to fill out a contact form and wait for a call back. Every hour between that form submission and your response is an hour they might sign up somewhere else. Real-time booking or a direct membership sign-up eliminates that friction completely.
Your page title is a tagline, not a search term
The browser tab for your homepage reads "Fueling Your Fitness" — which is great branding but tells Google nothing about who you are or where you are. People searching for a gym in Clayton aren't finding woodallsfitness.com because the site isn't optimized for those searches.
