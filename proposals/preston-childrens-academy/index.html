NAEYC & NC 5-Star Not Merchandised

Fewer than 10% of NC child care centers hold NAEYC accreditation. Your site references it but doesn't lead with it. Competitors with no accreditation still make their hero look prestigious — you actually have the credential and aren't showing it off.

Generic info@ as the Only Contact

A parent ready to book a tour on a Sunday night can't put a name or face to who they're reaching. No director-named inquiry path. No online tour scheduler. First-touch feels transactional, which undercuts the family-centered brand you've built in person.

No Waitlist or Parent Portal

Preston Children's Academy has the kind of reputation that generates waitlists. Right now, there's no visible waitlist signup or parent portal integration. Families who would gladly wait 6 months are instead calling back repeatedly — or worse, enrolling somewhere else after the first call.

Trust Signals Buried Below the Fold

Your James Jackson Ave location, Pam Holt's tenure, Amy Herring as Assistant Director, the small class sizes — none of it is on the hero. A parent who doesn't scroll never learns the strongest reasons to choose you.
