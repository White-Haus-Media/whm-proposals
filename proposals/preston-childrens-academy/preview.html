Our philosophy

The first five years shape a lifetime. We take that seriously.

At Preston Children's Academy, early learning isn't a product — it's a partnership with the families we serve. Our certified educators design each day around exploration, language, movement, and friendship, giving children in Cary, Apex, and western Wake County a foundation that lasts well beyond preschool.

That's why we pursue NAEYC accreditation (held by fewer than 10% of U.S. programs) and maintain the highest NC 5-star rating year after year. It's the same standard you'd want for your own child — because every child here is treated like our own.

From first words to first chapter books, each age group is led by educators who understand exactly what that year of life needs.
