Our Story

Family-Owned, Community-Rooted

Lillypads Child Development Center was founded in 2009 by Donna and Robert Naylor with one goal: to create a childcare center in Fuquay-Varina where every child is truly known and every family feels at home.

Seventeen years later, we hold a Four-Star North Carolina childcare license and continue to serve infants through school-age children from Fuquay-Varina, Holly Springs, and the surrounding communities.

We're not a franchise. We're neighbors — and we care about the families in this community the way neighbors do.

👑 Donna & Robert Naylor, Owner/Operators
